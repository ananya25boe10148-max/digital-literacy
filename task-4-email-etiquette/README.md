Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

| ✅ Do’s                                                           | ❌ Don’ts                                                      |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings                 | Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly       |
| Share accurate and verified information only                     | Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or misleading content       |
| Maintain a professional and respectful online presence           | Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments            |
| Think before posting to avoid inappropriate content              | Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown messages        |
| Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development | Do not spend excessive time on social media affecting studies |
